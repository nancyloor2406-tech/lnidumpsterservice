Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a dumpster rental cost nationwide?

Dumpster rental prices vary by state due to local disposal fees, fuel costs, and container availability. On average, prices range from $300 to $800. We offer competitive flat-rate pricing to ensure you get an affordable dumpster rental in your specific area.

2. What size dumpster do I need?

For small cleanouts or heavy dirt, a 10 yard dumpster is usually sufficient. For kitchen remodels or roofing, a 20 yard dumpster is standard. Large construction projects typically require a 30 yard or 40 yard dumpster. We can assist with dumpster sizing.

3. Can I put concrete in a regular dumpster?

No, heavy aggregates like concrete require specific concrete dumpsters near me to avoid weight overages. Mixing heavy materials in a standard rubbish bin rental can lead to safety issues and extra fees.

4. How long is the rental period?

Our standard rental period is generally 7 to 10 days. If you need the trash rental for a longer duration, simply let us know, and we can arrange a flexible extension for your project.

5. Is there a weight limit?

Yes, every container has a specific weight limit based on DOT regulations. Exceeding the limit on your trash bin rental will result in overage charges. We help you estimate the weight to keep your project on budget.
